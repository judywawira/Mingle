Mingle
Join us. Be a stranger.

Mingle is an attempt to create a twitter-ish service where users don't disclose their identity or relationships with other users (called follow, list, tag, etc. on various networks), in order to avoid behavioral targeting surveillance on individuals and groups.
The revolution will not be "liked".
Mingle is still a work in progress, but the demo server already allows chat between "fairly anonymous" users.
Note: [at least at the moment,] we're using Gravatar for user icons. It turns out that it can be a privacy risk (unless used wisely). If you can't be bothered to read the fine details, here's a video explaining how to safely join Mingle (first 1:15 minutes are granny-friendly, the rest is optional).
